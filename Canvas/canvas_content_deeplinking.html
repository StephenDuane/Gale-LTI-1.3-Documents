The following instructions are for deeplinking Gale content into your courses learning path. If you do not see any Gale Databases in your navigation pane. Please check in the navigation setting. If there are no databases there, please direct your Canvas Administrator to Canvas Installation Guide - Admin - LTI 1.1 or Canvas LTI 1.3 Admin Installation Guide

Navigate to the desired course you are wanting to deeplink Gale content into.

Click on Assignments.

Click on + Assignment.

Fill out the Assignment Name and click on plug in button.

Click on View All.

Select the Gale Databse.
