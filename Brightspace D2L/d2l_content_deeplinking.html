The following instructions are for deeplinking Gale content into your courses learning path. If you do not see any Gale Databases; Please direct your D2L/BrightSpace Administrator to D2L Brightspace LTI 1.3 Admin Installation Guide.
The instructions below are for adding a link to a Gale Article to your learning path.

Navigate to the course you are wanting to add Gale content into.

Click on Content.

Click Add a module... at the bottom of the screen. Provide a name for the module.

Under the module you just created. Click New.

Click New Assignment.

Click on the Insert Stuff button.
