The following instructions are for deeplinking Gale content into your courses learning path. If you do not see any Gale Databases; Please direct your D2L/BrightSpace Administrator to D2L Brightspace LTI 1.3 Admin Installation Guide.
The instructions below are for adding a link to a Gale Article to your learning path.

Navigate to the course you are wanting to add Gale content into.

Click on Content.

Click Add a module... at the bottom of the screen. Provide a name for the module.

Under the module you just created. Click New.

Click New Assignment.

Click on the Insert Stuff button.

Select the Database you are wanting to link content from.

Locate the article that you are wanting to link.

Click Link to Document.

Add the title to the Assignment Name. Click Save and Close when finished editing.

Locate the newly added assignment to the D2L course Learning Path.

The instructions below is for embedding a Gale Article to your learning path.

Navigate to the course you are wanting to add Gale content into.

Click on Content.

Click Add a module... at the bottom of the screen. Provide a name for the module.

Under the module you just created. Click New.

Click New Assignment.

Click on the Insert Stuff button.

Select the Database you are wanting to link content from.

Locate the article that you are wanting to link.

Click Link to Document.

If embedding, the user may get a pop up that says We blocked this content for you "Article Link" Do you want to allow it? Click the Allow button and make sure to always trust this URL checked.

Add the title to the Assignment Name. Click Save and Close when finished editing.

Locate the newly added assignment to the D2L course Learning Path. See the article is embeded into the Assignment.
